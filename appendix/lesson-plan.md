LEVEL 1
Meet Budgie the Budget Monster and begin your journey as a Money Trailblazer by exploring the basic concepts of how money works.
Lesson 1.1: What is money?
 Discover what money is, the different forms it comes in, and why we use it to trade for goods and services.
Lesson 1.2: Money through history
 Explore how people traded long ago and how money has evolved into the currencies we use today.
Lesson 1.3: How we use money
 Learn how we earn, save, and spend money, and how these steps help us plan for the future.
Lesson 1.4: Why money matters
 Find out why setting financial goals and planning for the short and long term helps us use money wisely.
Lesson 1.5: Needs vs wants
 Understand the difference between what we truly need and what we simply want so we can make smart spending choices.
Lesson 1.6: Understanding needs and wants
 Discover the different categories of needs and wants and how they can change as we grow.
Lesson 1.7: Making smart money choices
 See how good money decisions and strong habits can help us build a healthy financial future.

LEVEL 2
Meet Shappi the Savings Monster and get ready to explore key banking concepts and financial terms that will help you understand how money works.
Lesson 2.1: Banks and building societies
 Learn what financial institutions do, how they handle money, and why people use them.
Lesson 2.2: ATMs
 Discover how to use cash machines safely, what they can do, and their limits.
Lesson 2.3: Bank accounts – Different types
 Find out the differences between current and savings accounts and how to choose the right one.
Lesson 2.4: Interest – Money making money
 See how interest works and why simple and compound interest help your money grow.
Lesson 2.5: Digital money and online banking
 Explore how to manage money through apps and websites while staying safe online.
Lesson 2.6: Debit cards – Spending your money
 Learn how debit cards work and what features and limits come with using them.
Lesson 2.7: Credit cards – Borrowing money
 Understand what credit is and how to borrow money responsibly with a credit card.
Lesson 2.8: Credit scores – Your financial report card
 Discover how credit scores are calculated and what actions can raise or lower them.
Lesson 2.9: Inflation – Money’s changing value
 Learn how rising prices affect what your money can buy and how inflation impacts savings.
Lesson 2.10: Financial security – Keeping money safe
 Find out how to protect your personal and financial information and avoid common money risks.

LEVEL 3 – World Explorer
Meet Ciaran the Currency Monster and discover how money works around the world as you learn to travel, exchange, and manage money globally.
Lesson 3.1: What is currency?
 Discover what currency is and how different countries use money to buy and trade goods and services.
Lesson 3.2: Major world currencies
 Learn about the most widely used currencies and why they are important in global trade.
Lesson 3.3: Currency regions and unions
 Explore how some countries share the same currency and work together in financial unions.
Lesson 3.4: Exchange rates explained
 Find out how exchange rates work and how they affect the value of your money abroad.
Lesson 3.5: Strong vs weak currencies
 Understand what makes a currency strong or weak and how this impacts what you can buy.
Lesson 3.6: Currency exchange in practice
 Learn how and where to exchange money safely and get the best value when traveling.
Lesson 3.7: Using cards abroad
 Discover how to use debit and credit cards in other countries and avoid extra fees.
Lesson 3.8: Travel money management
 Learn how to plan, budget, and protect your money while exploring the world.
 
LEVEL 4 – Financial Pathfinder
Meet Tracy the Tracking Monster and master the skills of budgeting, planning, and building strong financial habits.
Lesson 4.1: Budget basics – Your financial foundation
 Learn what a budget is and how it helps you take control of your money.
Lesson 4.2: The 50/30/20 rule
 Discover a simple method for dividing your money between needs, wants, and savings.
Lesson 4.3: Needs vs wants – Making smart choices
 Learn how to tell the difference between essentials and extras to spend more wisely.
Lesson 4.4: Tracking your spending
 Find out how to monitor where your money goes and spot better saving opportunities.
Lesson 4.5: Creating a simple budget
 Learn how to build a basic budget that fits your lifestyle and financial goals.
Lesson 4.6: Understanding cash flow
 Discover how money moves in and out of your budget and how to keep it balanced.
Lesson 4.7: Setting SMART financial goals
 Learn how to set clear and achievable goals for your money and future plans.
Lesson 4.8: Emergency funds – Your financial safety net
 Find out why saving for emergencies helps protect you from unexpected costs.
Lesson 4.9: Automating your budget
 Learn how to use automatic tools to save, pay bills, and stay on track with ease.
Lesson 4.10: Regular budget reviews
 Discover why checking and updating your budget helps you stay in control over time.
 
LEVEL 5 – Wealth Wanderer
Meet Vinnie the Vault Monster and learn how to grow, protect, and secure your savings for the future.
Lesson 5.1: Pay yourself first
 Learn why saving before spending helps you build strong money habits.
Lesson 5.2: Savings strategies
 Discover different ways to save money and reach your financial goals faster.
Lesson 5.3: Understanding interest rates
 Learn how interest works and how it can help your savings grow over time.
Lesson 5.4: Specialised savings accounts
 Explore different types of accounts designed for specific saving goals.
Lesson 5.5: Tax-efficient savings (ISAs)
 Find out how ISAs help you save money while reducing the tax you pay.
Lesson 5.6: Digital banking features
 Discover useful online tools that make managing your money easier and safer.
Lesson 5.7: Banking security
 Learn how to protect your accounts and avoid scams and fraud.
Lesson 5.8: Financial protection schemes
 Find out how your money is protected if something goes wrong with a bank or purchase.
 
LEVEL 6 – Credit Conqueror
Meet Cardi the Credit Monster and learn how to borrow wisely, manage debt, and build a strong credit profile.
Lesson 6.1: Credit basics
 Discover what credit is and how borrowing money really works.
Lesson 6.2: Credit cards explained
 Learn how credit cards work and how to use them responsibly.
Lesson 6.3: Types of loans
 Explore different kinds of loans and what they are commonly used for.
Lesson 6.4: Understanding interest and APR
 Find out how borrowing costs are calculated and what APR really means.
Lesson 6.5: Credit scores and reports
 Learn how your financial behavior affects your credit score and record.
Lesson 6.6: Building credit responsibly
 Discover smart ways to build a positive credit history over time.
Lesson 6.7: Managing debt effectively
 Learn strategies to pay off debt and stay in control of what you owe.
Lesson 6.8: Credit protection and fraud prevention
 Find out how to spot scams and protect yourself from financial fraud.
 
LEVEL 7 – Prosperity Pioneer
Meet Fatima the Foresight Monster and plan ahead for life’s big moments and long-term financial success.
Lesson 7.1: Financial planning fundamentals
 Learn the key steps to creating a strong long-term financial plan.
Lesson 7.2: Setting SMART financial goals
 Discover how to turn your dreams into clear and achievable money goals.
Lesson 7.3: Life stage financial planning
 Explore how your financial needs change as you move through different stages of life.
Lesson 7.4: Retirement planning basics
 Learn why starting early can make a big difference to your future income.
Lesson 7.5: Protection planning
 Find out how insurance and protection plans help keep you and your family safe.
Lesson 7.6: Estate planning basics
 Discover how to plan what happens to your money and belongings in the future.
Lesson 7.7: Planning for major life events
 Learn how to prepare financially for big changes like moving, studying, or starting a family.
Lesson 7.8: Bringing it all together
 See how all your financial skills work together to build a secure future.
 
LEVEL 8 – Investment Explorer
Meet Gad the Growth Monster and begin your journey into investing and building long-term wealth.
Lesson 8.1: Investment fundamentals
 Learn the basics of investing and how money can grow over time.
Lesson 8.2: Understanding stocks and shares
 Discover how owning shares in companies can help build wealth.
Lesson 8.3: Understanding bonds
 Learn how bonds work and why people invest in them.
Lesson 8.4: Investment funds explained
 Explore how funds combine many investments into one simple option.
Lesson 8.5: Risk and diversification
 Find out how spreading your money can help reduce investment risk.
Lesson 8.6: Building an investment portfolio
 Learn how to choose and balance different investments to match your goals.
Lesson 8.7: Long-term investment strategies
 Discover smart strategies for growing your money over many years.
Lesson 8.8: Getting started with investing
 Learn the first steps to begin investing safely and confidently.
