## What the Proposed Curriculum Gets Right

- Needs vs wants appears early and is well placed
- Banking foundations (accounts, interest) are relevant and reasonably sequenced for YT
- Budgeting and goal-setting (Level 4) is the strongest match with research priorities
- Core credit literacy (Level 6) covers the right territory for V2 OT and YA

---


## Structural Divergence

The proposed curriculum is organised by **subject domain** (banking → budgeting → savings → credit → investing). The research recommends organising by **job to be done per segment** — starting with the immediate, emotionally-resonant problems each age group faces before introducing conceptual frameworks. This is not a content difference — it is a sequencing and framing difference that determines whether users engage or disengage in the first sessions.
